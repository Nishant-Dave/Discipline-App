About Discipline App

Discipline over motivation.

What is this app?

Discipline App is an accountability-focused platform designed for users who want to move past temporary motivation and build lasting, non-negotiable habits through structured task enforcement and consequence tracking.

How it works

1. Create

Define your non-negotiable tasks.

2. Complete

Finish tasks on time every day.

3. Proof

Upload mandatory evidence.

4. Consequence

Automatic penalties for failure.

Key Features

  • Non-negotiable recurring tasks
  • Severity-based consequence engine
  • Mandatory proof validation system
  • Performance and streak analytics
